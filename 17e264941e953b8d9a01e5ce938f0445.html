Why it is important to choose the right photos for a Dating site

Why it is important to choose the right photos for a Dating site
You won't get a second chance to make a first impression.
(c). Coco Chanel

The first impression is important both when Dating in real life and when making friends online. while chatting on a Dating site. You probably wouldn't write to someone whose photos you don't like. A profile photo plays a crucial role in helping you make the right first impression.

Let's figure out what to do to get as much attention from users as possible.

Not so a long time ago scientists conducted a study: how a photo affects your opinion of a person. 55 respondents were shown photos of different women: some of the ladies were smiling, while others had neutral or sad expressions.

The study participants were asked to answer several questions:

  • how much do they like it the person shown in the photo
  • would they have become friends and communicate with this person
  • what, in their opinion, is the character and type of personality of this person?

After a while, those who took part in the study "accidentally" met women, shown in the photo. They had no idea that they had seen these faces before. The meeting took place during a quiz game, during which new acquaintances had to learn about each other as much as possible.

Then, the respondents were again asked to describe the person with the photo.

Scientists have found that those who the woman was liked in the photo, and during the meeting we were already warm and positive towards her.

If the opinion was good, it did not change after the meeting. Those who were negative, unfortunately, also did not change their view of the person.

Thanks to the research, it was proved that a friendly facial expression creates a positive image of a person, which is difficult to erase later. After meeting in the real world, people keep a positive image in their subconscious, thereby they are already more attuned positively towards a new acquaintance.

It is also recommended to take photos in nature or on the street. They are more trustworthy.

People tend to maintain consistency in their judgments, which is why the participants in the experiment tried to prove that the first impression was correct. They led they behaved in a way that made the woman seem nice, tried harder to impress, and were friendly.

At the beginning of the experience, participants were willing to reconsider their views on women when they met them in person. It turned out the opposite. Respondents struggled trying confirm your opinion about the girl with the photo.

It is important to choose positive, friendly, and open photos for your online Dating profile. Someone who sees your photos will create a good first impression and stick to it until the end.
