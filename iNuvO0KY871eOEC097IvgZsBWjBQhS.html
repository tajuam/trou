5 types of men you will meet on a Dating site

Many women still believe that there are no adequate men on Dating sites. Alphonse, lazy, unemployed - nothing serious is definitely not to be found. This may have been the case 10 years ago. Fortunately, now more and more people are getting acquainted online, just because of the lack of time. We will tell you exactly what 5 types of men you will meet on a Dating site. Check it out?

1. Careerist

It is the careerists who install Dating apps due to lack of time. They have a regular schedule for every day: meetings, presentations, study, gym. These men are successful in all areas of life, except relationships. They don't have time to get acquainted. Therefore, a careerist creates a profile on a Dating site in the hope of meeting a kindred spirit.

He will not write 100 messages a day. Dry " Hello! What's up?"it will be enough for him. To interest you as a career woman, you can discuss online training, time management, and the secrets of dealing with constant stress.

If he understands that you think the same way, it will be easier for him to open up and talk about himself.

2. Merry

With such men it is easy. He the soul of the company, a lot of friends and acquaintances. As a rule, she posts photos with a wide smile or in a circle of friends. It is easy to communicate with fun people, they are attractive. If you manage to interest such a person, he will almost immediately offer to meet.
