How to behave on Dating sites: simple rules for guaranteed success

1. Determine the time

Many people will write to you, so set a period of one month, during which you will try to identify 3-4 of the most interesting people. You can meet with everyone, then decide who you want to continue with communication. Deactivate your profile for meetings so that other users don't distract you from your current business.

2. Ideal partner

Considering a candidate from another city or state? Always consider the temperament of a representative of another nation. Look at the photo and do you think that such a person will never pay attention to you? Do not take such thoughts into your head and write to him. Do not think for the person and do not decide for them how they will react to communication. In the process of Dating, it will be clear how they perceive you and assess possible relationships.
